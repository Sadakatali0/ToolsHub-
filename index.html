Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Tools Hub, we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don't ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don't share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law or protect our rights.
  • We don't store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of our services.

Information We Don't Collect

We designed Tools Hub to respect your privacy. Our tools work entirely in your browser:

  • Files Stay on Your Device - When you use our image, document, or text tools, your files are processed locally in your browser. We never upload them to our servers.
  • No Tracking - We don't use any analytics or tracking scripts that monitor your activity.
  • No Cookies - We don't use cookies to track or identify users.
  • No Accounts - You don't need to create an account to use our tools.

Information We May Collect

The only information we collect is:

  • Email Address - If you contact us via email, we'll have your email address only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.
  • Basic Server Logs - Like most website operators, our server automatically collects information that browsers typically make available, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. We don't link this information to any individual.

Third Parties

We don't share any information with third parties. We don't use any advertising networks or third-party services that would track our users.

Changes to This Policy

Although most changes are likely to be minor, we may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to our Privacy Policy.
